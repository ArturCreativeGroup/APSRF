# Research Origin

The APSRF project originated from a fundamental question:

> Can protocol ecosystems influence observable AI behavior without requiring access to model weights, hidden instructions, infrastructure, or internal architecture?

Initial experimentation focused on protocol-driven behavioral modification through contextual systems.

As the research evolved, the focus gradually shifted from behavioral influence toward protocol governance, protocol security, and agent ecosystem protection.
